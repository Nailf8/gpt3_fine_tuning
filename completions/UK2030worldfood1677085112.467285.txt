In 2030, artificial general intelligence (AGI) has been reached in the United Kingdom. This results in major changes in the world and food systems. The UK becomes the world leader in AI, and other countries begin to invest heavily in AI research and development. The UK's economy grows at an unprecedented rate, and its standard of living increases dramatically. People all over the world benefit from the UK's technological advances, and the world becomes a more peaceful and prosperous place. Food production becomes more efficient, and the quality of food increases. The world's population grows, and the demand for food increases. The UK's food system is able to meet this demand, and the world's food supply becomes more secure.
