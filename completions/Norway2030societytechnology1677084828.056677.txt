This includes manual labor, such as farming and manufacturing, as well as mental labor, such as data analysis and financial planning. This has led to massive unemployment, as there are simply no jobs left for humans to do. The government has responded by instituting a universal basic income, which allows everyone to live a comfortable life without having to work.
