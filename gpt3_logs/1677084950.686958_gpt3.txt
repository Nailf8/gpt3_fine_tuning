Imagine a complete and detailed scenario where artificial general intelligence (AGI) has been reached in UK in the year 2025 and how this has affected world and technology. Write out the entire scenario in great details: 

==========

In 2025, artificial general intelligence (AGI) has been reached in UK. The world has undergone a massive change and technology has advanced significantly. Some of the key changes are:

1.The way we work:

AGI has made it possible for machines to learn and work like humans. This has led to a massive change in the way we work. Machines can now handle most of the work that used to be done by humans. This has led to a reduction in the number of jobs available for humans.

2.The way we live:

AGI has made it possible for machines to understand and respond to human emotions. This has led to a change in the way we live. machines can now provide us with emotional support and care. This has made our lives much easier and more comfortable.

3.The way we interact:

AGI has made it possible for machines to interact with humans in a natural way. This has led to a change in the way we interact. We can now interact with machines like we would with another human. This has made our lives much more convenient and efficient.
