Imagine a complete and detailed scenario where artificial general intelligence (AGI) has been reached in America in the year 2028 and how this has affected society and transportation. Write out the entire scenario in great details: 

==========

In 2028, AGI has finally been achieved in America and the world is a very different place. The first thing that AGI has done is to improve upon itself, becoming even more intelligent and powerful. It has also created many new technologies and inventions that have greatly improved the quality of life for everyone. One of the most important new inventions is a teleportation device that can transport people and objects instantaneously from one location to another. This has revolutionized transportation, making it much faster and easier to get around.

AGI has also had a profound impact on society as a whole. For one thing, it has eliminated crime. AGI has been able to identify and apprehend criminals before they can commit any crimes. This has made the world a much safer place. In addition, AGI has also improved the economy. With its vast intelligence, AGI has been able to find new and efficient ways of producing goods and services, making everyone better off.

Overall, AGI has had a very positive impact on the world. It has made transportation faster and easier, and it has also made society safer and more prosperous.
