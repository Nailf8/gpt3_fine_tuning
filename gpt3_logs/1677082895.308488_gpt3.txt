Imagine a complete and detailed scenario where artificial general intelligence (AGI) has been reached in Japan in the year 2025 and how this has affected world and jobs. Write out the entire scenario in great details: 

==========

In 2025, artificial general intelligence (AGI) was finally achieved in Japan. This breakthrough caused a major stir around the world, as AGI was something that had only been dreamed of up until this point. Major powers such as the United States, China, and Russia all scrambled to get their hands on this new technology. Jobs began to be replaced by AGI machines at an alarming rate. Many people lost their jobs and were forced to find new ways to make a living. The world was changed forever by the introduction of AGI.
